A small, cross-platform habit tracker I built as the final project for a Mobile Development course at Dalhousie University, in the spirit of doing one thing well. No streaks designed to guilt-trip you, no social feed, no gamification, no notifications begging for attention. You name a habit, you check it off, and the app stays out of your way.

It was as much about restraint as features. The hardest decisions were the ones about what to leave out, which is a theme I keep coming back to in everything I make. A tool you actually keep using is usually the one that asked the least of you.
