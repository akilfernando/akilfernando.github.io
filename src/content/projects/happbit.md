A small, cross-platform habit tracker I built for myself, in the spirit of doing one thing well. No streaks designed to guilt-trip you, no social feed, no gamification, no notifications begging for attention. You name a habit, you check it off, and the app stays out of your way.

It was a personal project as much about restraint as features. The hardest decisions were the ones about what to leave out, which is a theme I keep coming back to in everything I make. A tool you actually keep using is usually the one that asked the least of you.
