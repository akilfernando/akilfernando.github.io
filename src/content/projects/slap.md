My first Global Game Jam entry, made solo while I was at Dalhousie. The theme was make me laugh, and I had almost no time that weekend, so I took the theme literally and kept the scope honest.

Slap is exactly what it sounds like. A big hand, a face on screen, and a counter. You tap to slap, the number goes up, and you keep slapping for as long as it stays funny. That is the whole game. The sprites are voxel art I made in MagicaVoxel, dropped into a small Unity project.

Silly, and meant to be. What I actually got out of it was the constraint. Start to finish it took two or three hours, because I was busy and that was all the weekend would give me. Shipping something small and complete in one sitting is a different muscle from building something large, and it is one worth keeping sharp. Sometimes the win is just that it exists, it runs, and it made someone laugh.

The itch.io page has the Android APK. Download it, install it on any Android phone, and slap away.
